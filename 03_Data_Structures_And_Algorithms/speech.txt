Establisted in 1995, Hung Vuong University of Ho Chi Minh City is one of the first non-public universities in Viet Nam. 
At Hung Vuong University of Ho Chi Minh City, learners play a central role, promoting the spirit of initiative, creativity and discovering their own abilities. 
The comprehensive educational values that Hung vuong University of Ho Chi Minh City bring to learners have sustainable value over time.
